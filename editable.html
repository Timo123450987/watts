Hernan Cattaneo presents Sudbeat showcase

To underground electronic music fans Hernan Cattaneo is nothing short of legendary. His dedication to not only creating the highest quality music but to empowering up-and-coming artists through his label Sudbeat has earned him a permanent place as one of electronic music’s most notable influencers. Expect a masterful music journey in a monumental industrial warehouse near the centre of Amsterdam.

The industrial Westergasfabriek holds 3 rooms plus a foodcourt and chill area. The impressive warehouse room will set stage to an 𝘢𝘭𝘭-𝘯𝘪𝘨𝘩𝘵-𝘭𝘰𝘯𝘨 set by Hernan Cattaneo. In the club room, Sudbeat will once again proof why this label has become almost as universally admired and adored as Hernan himself, with upcoming and established artists that fit the signature Sudbeat sound.
